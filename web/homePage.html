About Me

This site is part of my Web Engineering Class, and is where you will find all my projects for the class. In addition to this home page, you will find links that take you to my personal website, which you can find my portfolio, resume, contact information, and much more. I love programming. I love the challenge that it brings, the creativity that is needed, and that feeling of accomplishment when a project comes to an end. If you are like me, it's like a new child that you want to show off to everyone. A quote about programming that I love comes from the book “The Mythical Man-Month,” by Frederick P. Brooks, Jr. It says,

"The programmer, like the poet, works only slightly removed from pure thought-stuff. He builds his castles in the air, from air, creating by exertion of the imagination. Few media of creation are so flexible, so easy to polish and rework, so readily capable of realizing grand conceptual structures."

To explain a little more about me, I love the outdoors and spending time with my family. I spend as much time in the outdoors as I can. I enjoy hiking, camping, fishing, and hunting. I am also a convert of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. I have been a member for about 3 and half years now. I married my beautiful wife in the Rexburg Idaho Temple on April 9, 2016.
